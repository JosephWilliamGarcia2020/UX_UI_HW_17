How I Work

Asking Why: Why, Why, and WHY?

The most simple and provoking question is: why? Why do some
cultures think differently than others? Why are we using this flow
for this given task? The question of asking why can bring out the most mundane
answers, but best insights possible.

Using Quantitative and Qualitative Methods.

Surveys and statistical reports give great quantitativemeasures.
Interviews and observational fieldwork give great qualitative measures.
When put together, the possibilities of generating data for design is limitless.

Learn To Be Empathetic.

The phrase "You Have to Put Yourself in Someone Else's Shoes" is easier said than done.
To be empathetic is to truly listen to one's views and needs for the
sake of creating with purpose and drive.
